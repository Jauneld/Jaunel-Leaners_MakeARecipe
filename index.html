Spaghetti with Garlic Bread

Done by: Jaunel Deans


What you need
  • a box of spaghetti
  • a jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 pot
  • serving untensils
  • salt
  • water
  • seasoning
  • cheese
  • bread
  • butter
  • garlic
  • bowl

How to prepare Spaghetti
  1. In a large sauce pan, boil spaghetti with water until hot. Remove from heat and drain.
  2. While cooking, Add tomato sauce, paste, Italian seasoning, garlic, salt & pepper. Mix well. Simmer for 5-8 minutes. The remove from heat.

How to prepare the Garlic Bread
  1. Preheat oven to 425° and line a large baking sheet with foil.
  2. In a small bowl, mix softened butter with garlic, salt, and parsley until well combined.
  3. Spread cut sides of bread liberally with garlic butter. Sprinkle with cheese.
  4. Bake until bread is toasty and golden at the edges, about 10 minutes.
Enjoy

After you have created both meals, place the food on a plate. Then sprinkle more cheese on the meal. This meal with make you feel at home even if you are far from home. Enjoy this meal by yourself or make it for your family. It doen't matter when you eat it, it only matters if you enjoyed it.
